## Insert Timestamp

#### Steps

1. Tap the **“T” Text** button in the menu bar to enter the text menu.

2. Swipe left on the bottom menu bar and tap **“+”**.

3. Select **“Insert Timestamp”**, then tap **“Confirm”** to insert it.
