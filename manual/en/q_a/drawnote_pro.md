## **DrawNote Pro purchased but not working?**

As long as your device is signed in with the **same Google account used for the purchase**, you can restore Pro features.

⚠️ **If it still doesn’t work, please follow the steps below:**

1. Back up your files, then uninstall the DrawNote app.

2. Sign out of **all Google accounts** on the device (including accounts in the Play Store and browser).

3. Sign back in to the Play Store with the **correct Google account**, then reinstall DrawNote.

4. Open the DrawNote app and check the purchase status.

💡 **Tip:** Be sure to back up your files before uninstalling the app to avoid data loss.
